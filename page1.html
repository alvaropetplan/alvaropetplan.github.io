FAQs

  Why should you insure your dog?

Did you know that you’re more likely to claim on your pet insurance than your car or home policy? Unfortunately, pets are just as susceptible to illness and injury as we are. Insurance can help you ensure your dog gets the best care possible.

 How much does dog insurance cost?

There are a number of factors that affect how much insurance for your dog will cost, such as your dog’s age, breed and where you live. Our pet insurance are carefully calculated for each individual pet so why not get a quote and select the policy that suits your needs.

 Can I still purchase pet insurance if my pet has previously been ill?

If your pet has previously been ill or injured you can still purchase pet insurance. Before you take out a pet insurance policy you must inform us of any conditions, injuries and prior illnesses they've had and like all other insurance providers, unfortunately will not be able to cover you for these. These exclusions will be clearly stated on your certificate of insurance and will not affect the price of your premium.
